Forecasting Dashboard
Health service demand is volatile and hard to plan against. Live assumption sliders recalculate bed, roster and capacity pressure in real time, giving leaders a working model to plan surge and workforce decisions on. Proves forecasting logic and scenario design.

Data Risk Assessment
Ungoverned data quietly undermines every decision built on it. A structured scoring framework maps risk across identifier quality, coding, access and privacy, showing where remediation should be prioritised first. Proves risk assessment and governance thinking.

Business Case Builder
Good initiatives stall without a defensible funding case. This framework turns problem, options, cost-benefit and risk into a single recommendation built to support investment approval. Proves commercial framing and executive communication.

Deployment Strategy
Transformation programmes fail quietly when readiness and adoption risk go unmeasured. A governance dashboard tracks milestones, risk, budget and readiness in one view, built to support go/no-go and resourcing calls. Proves programme governance and risk synthesis.

Decision Matrix
Prioritising under resource constraint needs more than a gut call. Weighted criteria recalculate live as weights shift, giving leadership a transparent, defensible basis for choosing what goes first. Proves structured judgement and transparent criteria design.
