Capacity visibility reaches 74% by Q3 FY27 under Base Demand assumptions. The primary constraint is discharge delay, which limits usable bed capacity even when physical beds are available. The executive team should prioritise discharge coordination, timestamp quality, and bed management governance in the next planning cycle.
